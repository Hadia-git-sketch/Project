About Artist

Hadia Shabbir aka Diya

Hi, I'm Diya, an artist who loves to express creativity through Arabic calligraphy, mandala art, landscape sketching, doodling, and novel journal making. My work is inspired by emotions, culture, and imagination. Whether it's the beauty of flowing calligraphy or the intricate details of a mandala, each piece tells a unique story. Art is my passion, and I love sharing it with others. Thank you for visiting my gallery and being part of my creative journey!!!
